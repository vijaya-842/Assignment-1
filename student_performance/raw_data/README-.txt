Metadata for Student_Performance.csv

Data Description:

Columns, Data Type, and Description:

gender - Categorical: Represents the student's gender (male/female).
race/ethnicity - Categorical: Indicates the student's race or ethnicity, categorized into groups (A, B, C, D, E).
parental level of education - Categorical: Specifies the highest level of education attained by the student's parents.
lunch - Categorical: Denotes the type of lunch the student receives (standard or free/reduced).
test preparation course - Categorical: Indicates whether the student completed a test preparation course (completed/none).
math score - Numerical: The score the student achieved on the math exam (scale of 0-100).
reading score - Numerical: The score the student achieved on the reading exam (scale of 0-100).
writing score - Numerical: The score the student achieved on the writing exam (scale of 0-100).
